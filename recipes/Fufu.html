Fufu

Description

Fufu is an African swallow used for eating different African soups especially Nigerian cuisine. It is made from cassava and believed by many especially people

Ingredients

  • cassava
  • Plantain
  • Yam
  • Cow meat

Steps

  1. Peel Cassava and Plantain
  2. Wash with clean water and put on fire for 40minutes
  3. Remove from fire and separate the water,allow to cool for 10 minutes
  4. Get your motter and pistel and pound
  5. Your fufu is ready
  6. Fetch your light soup and there you go
