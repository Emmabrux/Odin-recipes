Rice

Description

When cooked, basmati rice grains are long, dry, and separate. They impart a pleasant, nutty aroma and flavor in any dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Stew

Steps

  1. Fetch a satisfied amount of water in a pan and put on fire, add salt
  2. After it boils for 7 minutes, add your plain rice and cover it
  3. Allow to boil for about 40 minutes and test its readiness
  4. Remove from fire and get your stew ready
  5. Your rice is ready to be served
